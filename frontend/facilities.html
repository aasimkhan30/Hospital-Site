Ambulance

The hospital has a well equipped ambulances. The basic task here is to retrieve emergency cases from the proximity areas of the hospital. The ambulances are equipped with advanced life support systems and carry a doctor on board for cardiac and other life threatening emergencies.

CAFETERIA

The hospital has a well equipped ambulances. The basic task here is to retrieve emergency cases from the proximity areas of the hospital. The ambulances are equipped with advanced life support systems and carry a doctor on board for cardiac and other life threatening emergencies.

AUDITORIUM

Auditorium is located in the basement floor of the hospital to conduct seminars, medical conferences and workshops. It has the capacity to host up to 80 participants. Multipurpose hall is located on the 13th Floor of the hospital for conducting mid size medical conferences and clinical workshops

GYMNASIUM

Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital offers the facility of Gymnasium located on the second floor of the hospital. There is a professional trainer to help you. The membership cost is Rs. 1430/- per month.

DAY CARE

Day care complex is on the 10th Floor. There are 9 beds with high end electronic monitoring systems. There is a dedicated team of highly trained nurses who manage the department. It is operational from 8 am to 8 pm.

FAMILY LOUNGE

Floor 11 has a family lounge that is meant for the patient and relatives to spend time together socially, read newspapers, surf the net and have tea or coffee. The view is breathtaking.

GENERAL STORES

General store is located on the ground floor of Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital. The Store has wide range of general items available. It includes magazines, books, packaged foods eatables, new born baby kit, mobile recharge coupons, etc.

ATM

ATM Service is available at the ground floor of the hospital. The ATM service is provided by AXIS Bank.

OUTPATIENT DEPARTMENTE

The hospital has very large Outpatient department complex located on the ground floor and the first floor. The outpatient departments have now expanded. On the third floor the OPDs are dedicated for Cardiology and also Human reproduction.

WHO WE ARE

Hospital, founded in honour of the legend, Padma Bhushan Dr Harshul Jadhav, came into existence in February 2004. The challenge of the early days was enormous, to foster the fledgling hospital into an enduring symbol of healthcare in the country. We recognised early on that to make a real and substantial contribution one had to redefine the standards themselves- instead of doing more of the same.
